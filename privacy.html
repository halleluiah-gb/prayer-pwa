Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Halleluiah Prayer Tracker web application (“App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”).

1. Information We Collect

With your consent, the app may collect the following limited information:

  • Your Google account email address (for authentication)
  • Your manually submitted prayer data

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to provide core app features, such as:

  • Recording and displaying prayer minutes
  • Saving this data securely in your Google Sheets
  • Sending password reset emails (if applicable)

3. Data Storage & Protection

We do not store your data on any external server. All user data is stored securely within your own Google account via Google Sheets. We use secure authentication methods and HTTPS encryption to transmit data. No sensitive information is shared or exposed publicly. Access to your data is strictly limited to the app functionality you've authorized.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained only as long as needed to provide functionality. You can delete your data at any time by removing or clearing the linked Google Sheet. You may also contact us at bijyjiby@gmail.com to request complete deletion, and your data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. All data stays within your account unless explicitly shared by you.

6. Requesting Minimal Scopes

The app requests only the minimum necessary access to your Google account (such as Sheets read/write access) to provide its core features. We do not request access to other services or broader scopes than required.

7. Your Consent

By using this app and authorizing access via your Google account, you consent to this privacy policy.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Updates will be published on this page with a revised date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at bijyjiby@gmail.com.
