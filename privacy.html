Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Halleluiah Prayer Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

This web app may collect the following information with your consent:

  • Your Google email address
  • Your prayer entries (as minutes or logs you submit)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Save your prayer log securely in your Google Sheet

All data is stored securely in your Google account (Google Sheets) and is never shared or stored on third-party servers.

Third-Party Access

This app does not share your data with any third parties. Your Google account information is used only to authenticate you and interact with your own spreadsheet.

Your Consent

By using this app and authorizing access via Google, you consent to this privacy policy.
