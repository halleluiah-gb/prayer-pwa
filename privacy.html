Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Halleluiah Prayer Tracker web application (“App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”).

1. Information We Collect

With your consent, the app may collect the following limited information:

  • Your Google account email address (for authentication)
  • Your prayer data (submitted by you manually)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to provide app features, such as:

  • Recording and displaying your prayer minutes
  • Saving this data in your Google Sheets
  • Sending password reset emails (if the feature is enabled)

3. Data Storage

We do not store your data on any external servers. All data is stored securely within your Google account (via Google Sheets), and only you have access to it.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Your data stays within your account and is only accessed as needed to provide functionality.

5. Your Consent

By using the Halleluiah Prayer Tracker app and authorizing access via Google, you consent to this privacy policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Updates will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact
