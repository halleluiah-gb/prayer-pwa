Welcome to Halleluiah Prayer Tracker

Purpose: Halleluiah is a simple web app designed to help you track the minutes you pray each day. You can submit, view, and review your prayer records over time.

Why We Request Google Access

We use Google Sheets to store your submitted prayer data securely. We require limited access to your Google account in order to:

  • Read/write your prayer log in a private Google Sheet
  • Send password reset emails if needed

Your data is never shared with third parties and is only used within this app for its intended purpose.
